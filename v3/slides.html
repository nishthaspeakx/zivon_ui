Zivon Voice UI · Design Rationale

Why these colors.

A deliberate journey across the color wheel — cool to warm, rest to reward. Every interaction ends warmer than it began.
Idle
Deep Indigo
"I'm here when you need me"
Calming, trustworthy, sustainable for hours on-screen. Same hue family Siri uses at rest.
Listening
Electric Cyan
"I hear you"
The universal listening cue. Google Assistant, Alexa, ChatGPT Voice — 20 years of user training in a single hue.
Thinking
Vivid Violet
"I'm reasoning"
The 2026 color of AI cognition. Anthropic, OpenAI, Perplexity all signal reasoning with violet.
Shipping
Warm Amber
"I delivered"
The only warm hue in the journey. Triggers a reward response — users feel the ship, not just read "done."
Cool
Warm
Blue → Cyan → Violet → Amber. Rest to reward. Every conversation ends warmer than it started.
Zivon Voice UI · Design Rationale

Why these animations.

Each motion is a metaphor the brain decodes in under a second. Color says what state. Motion says what that state feels like.
Idle
Slow breathing
"Alive, not waiting"
A breathing rhythm signals life without demanding attention. Static would read as frozen or disconnected.
Listening
Ripples outward
"Your voice, made visible"
The universal "sound spreading" metaphor — drop a stone in water. Reactive to the user's voice, so speech becomes motion.
Thinking
Neural swirl
"Visible cognition"
A literal cognition metaphor. Replaces a dead loading spinner with visible thought — users stop wondering if it crashed.
Shipping
Launch beam
"Delivered, not done"
Two-act delivery story — work assembles, then it launches. Upward motion = growth, momentum, the win moment.
Color tells you what state Zivon is in. Motion tells you what that state feels like.
